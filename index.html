About

I am a graduate film student at Yonsei University in Seoul, South Korea. I recently graduated from the University of California, Berkeley in May 2025 with a Bachelor's Degree in Film and Media.

My enthusiasm revolves around the art of storytelling — particularly expressed through the perspectives of people around the world. My current thesis work in graduate school revolves around Asian American cinema.

I have worked across roles including director, cinematographer, editor, and associate producer, spanning documentary, short fiction, and club productions in both the United States and South Korea.

Film Projects

Associate Producer

The Dao in Thao 2026

"The Dao of Thao is a feature documentary that follows comedian and solo performer Thao P. Nguyen as she creates her next one-woman show. Set largely in Stage Werx, an intimate, independent theater in San Francisco's Mission District, the film captures Thao over two years as she crafts new material shaped by the complexities of her life as a queer, Asian American refugee, daughter, wife, and mother."

Writing
